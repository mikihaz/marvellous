Meet & Greet

Want to hang out with your favorite Cartoon Network or MARVEL characters? Now's your chance. Head to the The Marvellous Meet and Greet zone to make friends with the full The Marvellous cast, including Spider-Man, Thor and even the Powerpuff Girls.

The The Marvellous VIP Experience Package includes:
' Access to our VIP lounge including selected refreshments.
' Dining reservations at a restaurant of your choice.
' Bookings and priority seating for certain shows and attractions, subject to availability.
' Exclusive 5-hour tour by a member of the Guest Relations team. If you wish to extend the tour hours, you will need to purchase a new package.
' Immediate VIP access to all rides and attractions.
' Should you wish to remain in the park post the 5 hour tour, each guest will be provided with an ultimate fast track passes.
' Tickets for our exclusive VIP Experience Package begins at Rs. 4995, with a minimum of 5 guests per group, and Rs. 999 for additional guests; with a maximum of 7 guest in a group. This is subject to availability for day of arrival.
*In case the number of guest exceeds the maximum number required you will be charged for an additional VIP package.

We are here to help so please get in touch if you have any questions.
